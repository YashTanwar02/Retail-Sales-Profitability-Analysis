# 📊 Retail Sales & Profitability Analysis

An end-to-end **Business Intelligence** project analyzing retail sales, profitability, customer behavior, and regional performance using the **Superstore dataset**.

Built with **Power BI, DAX, SQL, and Python**, this dashboard helps identify key business drivers and provides actionable insights for data-driven decision-making.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project answers important business questions such as:

- How are sales and profits changing over time?
- Which customers and products generate the most revenue?
- Which regions are the most profitable?
- Is the business experiencing sustainable year-over-year growth?
- Where can profitability be improved?

## 📂 Dataset

The project uses the **Superstore** retail dataset containing historical order-level transaction data.

**Key Features**

- Order Date & Ship Date
- Customer & Segment
- Product, Category & Sub-category
- Sales, Profit, Quantity & Discount
- Region & State

---

## 🛠 Data Preparation

- Cleaned and validated the dataset
- Created a Date Table for time intelligence
- Built a Star Schema data model
- Developed DAX measures for KPIs
- Implemented Year-over-Year (YoY) analysis
- Added interactive slicers and field parameters

## 💡 Key Insights

- Sales continue to grow, but profit remains volatile.
- A small group of customers contributes a significant portion of revenue.
- High sales do not always translate into high profitability.
- Several regions generate strong revenue but lower profit margins.
- Business growth varies across product categories.

---

## ✅ Recommendations

- Focus on improving profit margins instead of sales alone.
- Retain high-value customers.
- Optimize pricing and discount strategies.
- Develop region-specific business strategies.
- Invest in high-margin product categories.
